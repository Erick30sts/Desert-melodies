The Egyptian musical instruments were very advanced, different and had all the variety of musical equipment: air instruments, strings, and percussion. The musical equipment has changed and evolved over time, enriching the culture and contributing to festivals.

NEY

The "Ney" is an air instrument with 7 holes that can change the sound through the airflow. It is usually used to create "somber" melodies. One of the most antique flutes that existed.

HARP

The harp is one of the most antique musical instruments of ancient Egypt. The Egyptian harp is a large wooden mast with an arched soundboard, producing a melody by playing the strings. It is considered one of the most elegant musical instruments.
