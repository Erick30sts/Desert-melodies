Egypt is the home of many international and culturally influential artists in the music world, even from the classic melodies in ancient times, to the contemporary innovative fusions. The Egyptian music has the ability to reflect its history and diversity. This section is made to explore the lives of the Egyptian musical artists, those who left a mark in the music world.

SHA-AMUN-EN-SU

Was a figure in ancient Egyptian music, known for her ability to perform melodies that reflected the complexity and depth of Egyptian culture. Her musical work was very influenced by the religious and social traditions of the time. Described as a bridge between the human and the divine.

AMR DIAB

Is one of the most iconic and influential figures in contemporary Egyptian and Arab music in general. He has been in his career for over three decades, becoming a global symbol of modern Arab pop music by combining traditional Egyptian sounds with contemporary sounds and music styles.
